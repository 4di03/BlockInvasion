Welcome to Block Invasion!

    HOW TO PLAY
  • Avoid red squares(enemies) by shooting or dodging them
  • Pick up blue squars for ammo
  • Shoot enemies to regain health
  • Try not to stay in one place, as it is easier for enemies to target you that way.
  • Don't resize your window mid-game for the best experience
  • Don't Fall Off!
      • CONTROLS
    • W: Jump
    • A: move left
    • D: move right
    • V: Shoot left
    • B: Shoot Right
    • G: Shoot Up
